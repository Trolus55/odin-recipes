Pierogi

They come in many guises – minced meat, sauerkraut, mushroom, even blueberry – but perhaps they are most commonly filled with a deliciously starchy, creamy mix of potato and soft cheese.

Ingredients

  • 400g floury potatoes
  • Salt
  • 1 large white or brown onion
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 140g twaróg cheese
  • Pepper
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 250g maka puszysta
  • half tsp salt

Steps

  1. Put the whole unpeeled potatoes in a pan of well-salted water.
  2. Meanwhile, melt two tablespoons of the butter in a frying pan on a medium-low heat.
  3. Add the onion and a pinch of salt and fry.
  4. Drain the potatoes, put back in the hot pan to dry off further.
  5. Peel off and discard the skins.
  6. Pass the flesh through a potato ricer, or mash it.
